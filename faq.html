Faq

Question Related to Registration

What is the registration deadline ?
6 Dec 2013 would be deadline to register online.
Can I register directly at the venue ?
Yes you can but the rates applicable on the spot would be higher.
I have already registered but cannot attend the event. Can I cancel or transfer my registration ?
Cancellation charges are applicable 75 % amount is refunded. You can send substitution.
We are NASSCOM members. Do we get a discount for the conference ?
Yes, we have rates applicable for NASSCOM members.
Is the cost of food included in my registration fee ?
Yes food will be included with the registration fees.
What is the criteria for student registration ?
There is no criteria for students registration.

General Question

Do you have a list of hotels where I can stay?
Yes we have listed all the available hotels where you can stay. You can check the list of hotels on Venue page.
What can I expect of the climate in Noida? What clothes should I bring?
In Dec the temperature drops to 15- 10 Degree during day time , its advisable to carry heavy woollens cloths.
My query/question is not listed here. Whom shall I contact?
You can contact Shruti Verma at shruti@nasscom.in
Do you provide accommodation for participants from other cities?
Sorry we don’t . But we have preferred rates with hotels and guest rooms nearby for the conference.
Which hotels have free shuttle services to the conference center ?
We are still working on this.
What is there to do when I get there ?
You can check Explore page.

Question Related to Conference

Where do I pick up my name badges and registration bag ?
Registration desks are appointed in chronological order as per companies under paid and not paid desk. The staff on the ground can guide you to the appropriate desk.
Am I allowed to record sessions or presentations at the conference ?
No, you are not allowed to record the conference.
Will sessions and presentations be available for viewing after the conference ?
Yes all presentations and session videos would be available on our website post event.
What is the best way to get the updates of the conference ?
We are still working on this.
Will there be free wifi throughout the entire venue ?
Yes, we will be providing free wifi throughout the entire venue.
Do you collect feedback about the conference ?
Yes we will be collecting feedback about the conference.
Is there any Code of Conduct for the conference ?
Yes we do have Code of Conduct for the conference.
Is there any dress code for the conference ?
All the attendees are expected to come in business casuals.
Will you provide any writing pads, pens, etc or do we need to bring them?
Yes, we will be providing writing pads, pens etc.
Can I attend all the talks at NASSCOM ATC? I see in your ATC Schedule there are 5 rooms - A1, A2, A3, B1, B2. Is it possible to attend all the talks?
Since they are multiple tracks at the same time, a delegate can choose to attend the session of your interest. All the sessions would be recorded and can be viewed after the event.
