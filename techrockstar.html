How to participate in the awards?

NASSCOM nominates individuals from the teams who have contributed significantly to the product/company and to the technology industry. We also welcome nominations via referrals.

How are the winners selected?

The list of the nominees will be scrutinized by an esteemed jury, who will make a decision after weighing in all the aspects. Prior to being presented to the jury and the beginning of the voting, the list of the nominees will be presented before the technology community.
The awards are categorized into two categories, jury awards and people’s choice awards. Public voting will be closed before the start of the ATC conference. Awards will be presented to the first and second positions.

What is the importance of this award?

The names of the winners will feature in a press release of NASSCOM. This award will guarantee an enhanced presence on acclaimed media forms like, TV, print, newspaper, radio, internet, etc. In addition to the increased media attention, NATC will also acknowledge the winners and second placed individuals or groups. Developers who reach the final stage of the Tech RockStar awards will receive one complimentary pass of the event.
