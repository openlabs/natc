Quantum The Leap:

Formerly known as Elevate, this nightclub is a congregation haven of party lovers in Noida. The vote is still out about its status as the best nightclub in NCR region.

Located next to the nightlife district of Noida, this shopping avenue comprises of everything you need.

For all the food lovers, this place offers a carnival to the taste buds. Located at a convenient distance from the ATC venue, it offers good value for money.
